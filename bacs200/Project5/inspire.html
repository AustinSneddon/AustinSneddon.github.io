Denis Villeneuve

As someone interested in the film industry I am greatly inspired by
Denis Villeneuve. I find his interest in making films he know wont be
wildly popular, but still decidedes to make them endearing. I also do
love them films he does end up making.

Denis Villeneuve has a few charecteristics that inspsire me most.

  • Persistance in making something to a great quality.
  • Recognizing something didn't do well [financially], but being okay with it.
  • Ability to create something beautfiully made.

Denis Villeneuve is an udoubtable force in the film industry today. He isn't making moveis
that are pretentious (for the most part), but movies that are accessable for everyone. He
has a great desire to get people in movie theaters and to enjoy the art that him and many
others create.
